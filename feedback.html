Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and programs

Do I need to be in shape to join?

Absolutely not! Our programs are designed for all fitness levels, from complete beginners to advanced athletes. Our trainers will work with you at your current fitness level and help you progress safely.

What should I bring to my first session?

We recommend bringing comfortable workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a towel. We provide all necessary equipment. If you have any specific medical concerns, please bring relevant documentation.

How often should I work out?

This depends on your goals, current fitness level, and schedule. Most beginners start with 3 days per week, while more advanced individuals might train 4-6 days. Our trainers will recommend a schedule that works for you.

Can I cancel or freeze my membership?

Yes, we offer flexible membership options. You can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical reasons or travel. Cancellation policies vary by membership type - please contact us for details.

Do you offer virtual training sessions?

Yes! We offer both in-person and virtual training options. Our virtual sessions are conducted via video call and include personalized programming, form checks, and nutrition guidance just like our in-person sessions.

How do I know which program is right for me?

We recommend starting with a complimentary fitness assessment. One of our trainers will evaluate your current fitness level, discuss your goals, and recommend the best program to help you achieve results.
