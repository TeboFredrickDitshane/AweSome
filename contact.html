Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with AweSome?

Simply sign up for a free account, set your wellness goals, and start tracking your habits. Our onboarding process will guide you through everything you need to know.

Is AweSome free to use?

We offer both free and premium plans. The free plan includes basic habit tracking and access to our workout library. Premium plans include advanced analytics and personalized coaching.

Can I sync AweSome with other fitness apps?

Yes! AweSome integrates with popular fitness trackers and health apps to provide a comprehensive view of your wellness journey.

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

Do you offer customer support?

Our support team is available Monday through Friday to help with any questions or issues you may have.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your premium subscription at any time. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
