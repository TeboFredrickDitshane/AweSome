About AweSome

Empowering individuals to achieve their wellness goals through innovative technology and expert guidance.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, AweSome was born from a simple yet powerful idea: wellness should be accessible, achievable, and sustainable for everyone. Our founder, struggling with maintaining healthy habits in a busy world, couldn't find a platform that combined effective habit tracking with expert wellness guidance.

After trying countless apps and programs that fell short, we decided to create the solution we wished existed. AweSome brings together cutting-edge technology, evidence-based wellness practices, and a supportive community to help you build lasting healthy habits.

Today, we're proud to be helping thousands of people transform their lives, one habit at a time. Our mission remains the same: to make wellness achievable for everyone, regardless of where they are in their journey.

Our Mission

To empower individuals worldwide to achieve their wellness goals through innovative technology, expert guidance, and a supportive community that makes healthy living accessible and sustainable.

Our Vision

A world where everyone has the tools, knowledge, and support they need to live their healthiest, most fulfilling life, creating a global community of wellness-focused individuals.

Our Values

Community First

We believe in the power of community support and shared experiences in achieving wellness goals.

Innovation

We continuously innovate to provide the most effective and user-friendly wellness solutions.

Holistic Wellness

We approach wellness from all angles - physical, mental, emotional, and social well-being.

Trust & Privacy

We prioritize user privacy and data security, building trust through transparency and reliability.
