Our Story

Helping you achieve your fitness goals, one workout at a time.

Who We Are

AweSome was founded in 2020 by a group of fitness enthusiasts who recognized the challenges busy students and professionals face in maintaining a consistent fitness routine.

Our mission is to make high-quality fitness guidance accessible anytime, anywhere. Whether it's midnight study sessions or early morning workouts, we've got you covered.

What started as a small project to help friends has grown into a platform serving thousands of members worldwide, all united by the goal of living healthier, more active lives.

Our Core Values
