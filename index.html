Our Story

Founded in 2025, AweSome was born from a simple idea: wellness should be accessible to everyone. Our founder struggled with maintaining healthy habits and couldn't find a platform that provided both tracking capabilities and expert guidance.

We built AweSome to be the all-in-one wellness platform we wished existed. Today, we're helping thousands of people transform their lives through better habits and personalized wellness journeys.
